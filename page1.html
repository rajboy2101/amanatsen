CHAIRMAN

Dato Hj Mohd Aris Bin Hj. Ramli was born on 13th September 1954 in Alor Gajah, Melaka. Obtained early education in Singapore up to HSC education.

He started his service in the PDRM on 1 September 1973 and completed his police training on 30 August 1974. He started his service in Bn. 2 PPH and later became a trainer at the Forest Police Force Training School in Ulu Kinta until 1979. He then started the work of an investigating officer in Ipoh and later as an area Inspector in Pekan Baru. He served as KPD (J) in Kampar in 1988 and in 1995 took over as T/OCCI in Terrenganu.

In 1998 attended a DPS course and subsequently served as KBSJD North East in Penang. Next as KPD Kubang Pasu, Kedah in 2002. In 2005 it was changed to Kuala Lumpur as ACP KJSJK. In 2009, obtained the DIMP medal and was appointed as T/Director of JSJK until September 2014.
He has successfully obtained the rank of DIMP with the title of Dato in 2009. He also obtained a Master's Degree from MHRM from University Utara Malaysia.

EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR

Encik Permal Kunder A/L Karupan has served in the Ministry of Education Malaysia as a Trained Teacher for 8 years at Jalan San Peng Secondary School, Kuala Lumpur. He then ventured into the private sector from 1998. Among the fields he ventured into and experience are Construction, Property Management, Building Management, Finance Manager, Executive Director and as a director and advisor of several private companies.

He holds a Masters in Business Management from Kuala Lumpur Infrastructure University College (2010) and an Advance Diploma in Business Administration from Rima College Group (1995). With extensive experience and knowledge in this corporate field he will be able to make Amanats Security Sdn. Bhd. A dynamic and developed company.

ADVISOR

Datuk Muhammad Fuad Bin Talib {SAC (retired)} has served in the Royal Malaysia Police Force for 37 years. He has held several positions including Tumpat Police Station Commando Officer, Terenganu District Deputy Chief of Police and other important positions.

He holds a Masters in Science from Universiti Pertanian Malaysia (2002) and a Diploma in Police Science from Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia (1997). With this vast experience, he will be able to give advice and further make the management of Amanats Security Sdn Bhd efficient and skilled.
